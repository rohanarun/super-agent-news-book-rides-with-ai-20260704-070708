How booking rides with AI works

1) Ask in plain language

Tell Super where you’re going and any constraints (budget, car size, arrival time). This is similar to ChatGPT or Gemini for intent capture.

2) Compare options

Super opens the web or apps to compare ETAs and prices. Unlike Siri or Grok, it can operate the browser when needed.

3) Confirm details

Pickup spot, rider notes, and timing are reviewed before any action. You approve each step.

4) Reuse preferences

Your frequent routes, pickup quirks, and account flows are remembered in a computer-use cache, so repeat trips are faster and cheaper over time.
